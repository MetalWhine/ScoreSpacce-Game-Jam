You are a germ -> find tutorial for customizing germ
Get injected into cat

Fight immune cells
White blood cells -> just follows the player -> 100 points -> -10
Neutrophils -> aggresive suicide bomber -> 500 points -> -30
Helper T Cell -> shoots antibodies -> 1000 points -> -10
Macrophages -> invincible but very slow and turns proteins to super proteins (Can only have 1 exist)

Bacteria is constantly running out of protein
100 protein
loses 1 protein/second
if hits 0 -> death
Protein can be picked up from dead enemies -> 100 points each
Super proteins -> 1000 points each

There are combo chains that multiplies your built up score
At the end of the game, depending on your score, you can see how badly you fuck up the cat
